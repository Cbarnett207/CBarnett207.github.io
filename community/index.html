CryoCommunity

The product of the multi-institution Midwest and Random Stragglers (MARS) URGEgeosciences pod, CryoCommunity is a community driven intiative within the polar sciences that provides plethora of information of best practices for accessibility and safety within the geosciences. CryoCommunity houses a wide range of documents and information covering topics that range from field safety protocols and inclusivity plans while attending conferences, to student mentoring and educational websites for teaching. Please check out their website for the latest updates, and you how can make the polar and geoscience communities a more acessible and safer place for everyone.
