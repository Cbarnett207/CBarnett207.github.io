Research

Throughout my academic career my research interests have been varied between multidisciplinary projects within the oceangraphic and cryospheric sciences. My current research focuses on quantifying complicated ocean-cryosphere interactions through observational remote sensing techniques. Using NASA Operation IceBridge (OIB) radar depth sounder imagery, I compute submarine melt rates for ice shelves throughout northern Greenland.
